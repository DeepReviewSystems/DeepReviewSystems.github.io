Abstract Screening Services for Systematic Reviews

At DeepReview Systems LLC, we specialize in providing abstract screening services to support systematic reviews. Our unique cutting-edge AI and machine learning approaches streamline the review process, enabling faster and more accurate screening of vast amounts of research literature. We offer solutions for different types of systematic reviews to cater to your specific needs.

Our Screening Services

  • New Systematic Reviews

    If you're conducting a completely new systematic review, we provide comprehensive abstract screening to help identify relevant studies efficiently. Our service includes end-to-end support for delivering a fully categorized list of screened abstracts. This service is ideal for researchers, healthcare professionals, and organizations embarking on new evidence synthesis projects.

  • Updated Systematic Reviews

    For clients updating a previous systematic review, we offer tailored screening services that incorporate your existing data. We focus on identifying newly published abstracts that meet the established criteria, ensuring that your systematic review remains up-to-date with the latest research. By leveraging existing labeled data, this service is cost-effective and helps accelerate the review process.
